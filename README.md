### AR Industrial Emergency Training Simulator
---

An interactive Augmented Reality (AR) based emergency training simulator designed for industrial safety training. This web-based system allows users to simulate emergency scenarios such as fire hazards, gas leaks, electrical faults, and mass evacuations inside a factory environment.

The simulator helps trainees learn correct emergency response procedures through an engaging visual interface with hazards, alerts, evacuation routes, and interactive actions.

---

#### Project Overview
Industrial workplaces require proper emergency preparedness. Traditional training methods often lack realism and engagement.

This project provides a browser-based AR-style simulation that helps users practice emergency management by:

- Identifying hazards  
- Following emergency procedures  
- Taking correct safety actions  
- Learning evacuation routes  

The system evaluates user actions using timers, scoring, and scenario-based tasks.

#### How It Works

1. Select an emergency scenario from the control panel.  
2. Hazards will appear inside the factory layout.  
3. Follow the AR guidance instructions.  
4. Perform the correct emergency actions:
   - Sound alarm  
   - Use fire extinguisher  
   - Evacuate personnel  
   - Call emergency services  
   - Emergency shutdown  

Complete the scenario before time runs out.

Your performance is evaluated using **score, progress bar, and completion time**.

#### Emergency Scenarios

#####  Fire Emergency
Fire detected near industrial machinery.

Actions required:

- Sound alarm  
- Use fire extinguisher  
- Evacuate personnel  
- Call emergency services  

---

#####  Gas Leak Emergency
Gas leakage detected in machine area.

- Emergency shutdown  
- Activate alarm  
- Evacuate workers  
- Call emergency services  

---

#####  Electrical Hazard
Electrical fault detected in power system.

- Shutdown power  
- Sound alarm  
- Evacuate area  
- Contact emergency team  

---

#####  Mass Evacuation
Multiple hazards require complete facility evacuation.
